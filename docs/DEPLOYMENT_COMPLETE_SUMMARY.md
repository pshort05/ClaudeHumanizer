### 16 Priority 1 Sections Implemented

1. **physical_tells_patterns** - 35 patterns
   - Trembling hands, breath catching, jaw clenching, etc.
   - Detection: Mechanical body-part verbs in emotional context

2. **emotion_intensifier_bans** - 14 patterns
   - Paper-thin, gossamer, threadbare, stretched to breaking
   - Detection: Texture/fragility language with emotion nouns

3. **transition_beat_placeholders** - 15 patterns
   - For a moment, for a beat, after a moment, etc.
   - Detection: Temporal delay phrases between actions

4. **gaze_descriptors** - 22 patterns
   - Assessing, unreadable, inscrutable, cataloguing, etc.
   - Detection: Abstract eye descriptors implying depth

5. **temperature_emotion_bans** - 20 patterns
   - Cold dread, heat pooled, fire licked up, frozen, etc.
   - Detection: Temperature metaphors for emotion

6. **breaking_cracking_metaphors** - 17 patterns
   - Something broke, cracked, shattered, fractured, etc.
   - Detection: Structural failure metaphors for emotions

7. **anchor_tether_metaphors** - 10 patterns
   - Tethered to, the only thing keeping, anchored to, etc.
   - Detection: Passive dependency metaphors

8. **edge_precipice_metaphors** - 13 patterns
   - On the edge of, teetering, barely holding on, etc.
   - Detection: Abstract geography for emotional state

9. **time_moment_phrases** - 13 patterns
   - Time stretched, suspended in amber, world narrowed to, etc.
   - Detection: Cinematic time suspension language

10. **permission_allowance_patterns** - 11 patterns
    - Allowed herself to, permitted himself to, gave permission to, etc.
    - Detection: Self-permission constructions

11. **realization_understanding_patterns** - 14 patterns
    - Clicked into place, clarity crashed over, landed with force, etc.
    - Detection: Sudden understanding tells

12. **danger_threat_descriptors** - 19 patterns
    - Lethal grace, predatory stillness, coiled to strike, etc.
    - Detection: Predator/animal danger vocabulary

13. **possession_claiming_phrases** - 13 patterns
    - Staked a claim, branded into, marked, written into skin, etc.
    - Detection: Branding/ownership metaphors

14. **religious_exclamations** - 10 patterns
    - Oh god, god yes, jesus, christ, lord, etc.
    - Detection: Reflexive religious filler

15. **ending_cliches** - 12 patterns
    - For now that was enough, everything had changed, etc.
    - Detection: Summary closures that label impact

16. **description_cliches** - 16 patterns
    - Orbs, pools, mane, column of neck, sharp planes, etc.
    - Detection: Formulaic descriptor templates

### Standardized Structure

All sections follow master list standardization:
- Description: Why this pattern matters
- Detection rule: How to identify it
- Common patterns: 10-35 pattern variations each
- Allowed replacements: What to use instead
- Replacement strategy: Preferred vs. alternatives
- Examples: Bad AI writing vs. good human writing
- Pattern instruction: Specific detection guidance
